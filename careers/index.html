We are a small big organisation. Small in number and big in intent. We live what we stand for. That helps us stay grounded, inclusive, flat and driven. Everyone on our team is an emblem of our culture.

Goal Setting

For our goal setting, we use the OKR process.

O = Objective is an outcome

KR = Key Results are the milestones to deliver that outcome.

There should be 2-3 measurable key results for every objective.

We define our tangible objectives in three categories.

To deliver customer and business outcomes

To deliver growth and development for self

To deliver growth and development for self

We do not follow a formal review process. 

Every individual is expected to be a committed self-starter and driver!
